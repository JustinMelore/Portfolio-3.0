Our Light

Though the world may be darkened and corrupted by loneliness, a simple act of kindness can bring light to a gloomy world. As you face the challenges and puzzles of life, remember that kindness can be returned. Our Light is a puzzle game that plays into the theme of sharing your light.

Play Game
Platform: Windows
Engine: Unity
Development Duration: January - May 2025
Project Role: Lead Engineer

In Our Light, the player travels through a gloomy landscape using the special light from their lantern to overcome obstacles, such as creating bridges of light to cross gaps or clearing out dense fog. However, your light is limited, and if you do not use it carefully, you will succumb to the darkness around you. Luckily, you do not have to go alone, as there are others you can meet who will help share your burden if you share your light with them.
